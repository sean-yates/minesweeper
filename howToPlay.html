How to Play Minesweeper

Minesweeper is a game of logical deduction, where you have a grid board with an unknown number of hidden mines that you must avoid. The entire board starts unrevealed, and you can reveal a square by clicking it (or tapping on mobile). The first square you click will never be a mine, nor will the squares next to it. The board will then automatically reveal any squares adjacent to a square that isn't next to any mines, and squares that are next to any number of mines will show how many they are next to. The flag stops us from clicking it again unless we remove the flag by right clicking the square again. We can also use the flags to reveal squares in another way: by clicking a square that is already revealed.
